DIEGO IBARRA-ASIAIN
Coder - Learner - Musician

I'm Diego, a professional musician with over a decade's experience as a session bass player. Now, I'm embarking on a new journey - exploring the world of coding.

My journey began when I was just 14, creating simple websites on topics close to my heart, like football, the NBA, and music. Back then, HTML was quite different, and I relied on Dreamweaver for my projects.

Today, at 35, I'm excited to blend the familiar with the uncharted. Coding is my newfound fascination, and I'm determined to make the most of it.

I initially tried to join UK government-backed bootcamps but quickly realized I had no prior coding knowledge. With determination, I embarked on self-learning using resources like YouTube, FreeCodeCamp, and Scrimba. Gradually, I began to grasp the intricacies of coding, and it no longer felt unfamiliar.

I discovered Founders and Coders (FAC), renowned as one of the top programs in the UK. Although I had attempted to join FAC in the past, my knowledge was not yet sufficient. I'm still on my learning journey.

Attending FAC meetings, I found myself welcomed in an environment filled with people ready to learn and help. It became clear that FAC is where I want to be to continue my learning journey.

As I move forward, I'm enthusiastic about embracing the vast opportunities in web development. Coding represents the perfect blend of creativity and problem-solving, and I'm determined to make a meaningful contribution in this dynamic field, all while aspiring to combine my passion for music with programming in the future.
